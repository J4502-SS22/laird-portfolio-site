Hi! I’m Skylar Laird, a reporter and assistant city editor for the Columbia Missourian in Columbia, MO. I am a student at the University of Missouri – Columbia studying print and digital news reporting. I’ve worked a variety of positions at several different news outlets. I am passionate about journalism and believe all people should have access to information about what’s happening in their communities and their worlds.


I currently live in Columbia, Missouri, with my pet axolotl, Gene. When I’m not working, you’re likely to find me writing, reading or planning my next game night.
